Dynamic Family Mapping

FamilyValue empowers you to represent your family exactly as it is, no matter how beautifully complex. Our system is designed for:

  • Unlimited Members: From nuclear to vast extended families, add everyone who is part of your unit.
  • Clear Relationship Definition: Specify how each member is related (mother, father, gogo, cousin, uncle, adopted child, guardian, even non-blood relations like a 'family friend being supported' linked through a specific member).
  • Visual Family Organogram: See your family structure come to life! The tree dynamically updates as you add members, showing genetic ties (e.g., "1st degree," "2nd degree") and connections.
  • Multi-Household Support: Easily manage members across different households, crucial for co-parenting or extended families living separately but connected.

This ensures that every individual's place and connection within the family is acknowledged and understood.
