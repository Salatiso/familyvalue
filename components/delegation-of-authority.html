Delegation of Authority Charter

By creating a Family Hub, you, as the Administrator, agree to this Delegation of Authority. This establishes a clear governance structure to protect all members and the family's integrity.

1. The Administrator Role:
  • You have the primary responsibility for inviting members and maintaining the family structure.
  • You initiate the "Handshake Protocol" for releasing public information.
  • You act as a default member of the Executive Team.
2. The Family Council Chairperson Role:
  • Acts as the final moral and ethical guide for the family.
  • Holds the final vote on all requests to make sensitive family or individual information public.
  • Their approval is mandatory for key structural changes.
3. The Executive Team Role:
  • Members act as co-approvers for information release requests.
  • A minimum of one Executive Member (in addition to the Chairperson) must approve a release request.
  • They provide strategic oversight for the family's ventures and public brand.
4. Guardian Role:
  • Any member designated as a parent or guardian for a minor has the initial authority to approve or deny that minor's requests to share information.
  • A Guardian's approval is required before a minor's request can proceed to the Executive Team.

This charter ensures that the release of information is always a conscious, collaborative, and secure process. It can be amended in the future with unanimous consent from the full Executive Team and the Family Council Chairperson.
